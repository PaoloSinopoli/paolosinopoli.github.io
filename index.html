About

I am an undergraduate researcher at the Ohio State University, where I work with Dr. Anita Hopper to study tRNA biology. Previously, I was a 2025 summer intern at Scripps Research with Dr. Megan Ken & Dr. Jamie Williamson, and a 2024 summer intern at the National Cancer Institute with Dr. Sandy Wolin. I am broadly interested in the fundamental processes that underlie life.

I am fortunate to be supported by the Barry M. Goldwater Foundation, Pelotonia Cancer Research Foundation, and various institutional funding sources.
